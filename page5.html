3D Print

Bringing Kirby to life with the help of 3D printing.

The cultural significance of this piece is gaming culture. Gaming culture has made and is continually making a huge impact on society so I had chosen the infamous pink ball, Kirby, to represent it. Out of all the iconic Nintendo characters, I had always had a soft spot for Kirby.
This piece was designed in Minecraft and using the g-code, Kirby was brought to life with 3D printing.
