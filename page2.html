Glitch Art

Overall the theme of this series is "lost in translation".

The first image was rendered through Audacity. With the second image, I used TextEdit to render the glitches. In today's society, we rely on technological devices to communicate with one another. The original image consists of a gathering of hands holding their mobile devices and I had wanted to communicate a sense of disconnection with the shift in lines. Although you see this couple looking only at each other in this sea of people, you feel that there is a disconnect between the two as the man's face is distorted.
Through devices, we can write so many words that only say so little about one's true feelings. The couple represents the same disconnection as one of them has failed to be honest about their thoughts and feelings.

LaCorgi is my first project which involved rendering images through Audacity.
